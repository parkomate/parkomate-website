WHO WE ARE

Our company history and philosophy

Parkomate by Knowhassles Ideation and Solutions is a Mumbai based Sensor based parking solutions provider company founded in 2017. We use sophisticated state of art technology that aims at improving the parking hurdles faced by the ones managing the parking lots and vehicle owners on daily basis. Parkomate’s solution experts along with its technical team have designed cost effective and innovative array of products. This has over the time made it a lot easier to maintain the parking area, with giving the entire establishment a futuristic and technologically advanced outlook. We specialize in delivering end-to-end solutions including Designing, Developing, Supply, Installation, Testing, and Commissioning of all kinds of parking sensors and services. Our vision is to revolutionize businesses that provide parking service and to exhibit how shifting from traditional parking and maintenance to the new sensor-based model helps generate more revenue.
